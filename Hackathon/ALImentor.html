ALImentor

Congratulations! You have moved to Israel. If you are feeling a bit lost, we am pleased to let you know that you your not alone.

ALImentor is proud to say "We've got your back!" Our mission is to provide Olim Hadashim (new immigrants to Israel) a helping and guiding hand by setting them up with a personal Aliyah mentor who will be with them every step of the way. Our mentors are the go-to person for any assistance needed.

ALImentor works together with the Misrad haKlita to provide free service to all olim hadashim.
